Custom Guitars

Instructions for Use

Here at NUZ Guitars, we pride ourselves on the custom side of our guitar sales. That's why we've given you the ability to design your dream guitar through our website, all from the comfort of your home.


You simply need to select the desired neck and body from te provided lists, and a preview of what that guitar would look like will be generated. We even have over 200 possible guitars for you to design.


DISCLAIMER: this function is currently in alpha; some features, such as further detail than a body and neck are unavailable until further notice.
